# Geskap App - Complete Features Guide

## What is Geskap?

Geskap is a complete business management system designed to help small and medium businesses manage their entire operation from one place. Whether you run a single store, multiple locations, or an online shop, Geskap helps you track sales, manage inventory, handle orders, and understand your business performance.
